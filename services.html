Detailed Services

Economic Modelling & Forecasting

  • Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) and Input–Output (I–O) modelling to evaluate government policy, taxation, subsidies, and investment impacts.
  • Econometrics and forecasting for macroeconomic indicators, mineral demand, and commodity prices.
  • Mineral market analysis and forecasting, including Herfindahl–Hirschman Index (HHI) for competition assessment.

Value: Evidence-based insights into how policies and projects shape PNG’s economy.

Project Appraisal & Investment Analysis

  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) and Net Present Value (NPV) evaluation of mining, energy, and infrastructure projects.
  • Cost of capital & discount rate analysis (sovereign and financing risk; WACC and structuring).
  • Decision trees to compare risk-neutral and risk-averse investment pathways.
  • LCOE-linked appraisals where energy cost drives project economics.

Value: Transparent, robust investment appraisals aligned to real-world risk.

Modern Asset Pricing & Real Options Valuation

  • Modern Asset Pricing (MAP) to capture commodity volatility and project risk.
  • Real Options Valuation (ROV) for managerial flexibility (expand, delay, suspend, abandon).
  • Use of stochastic models, binomial lattice methods, and replicating portfolio approaches.
  • Address DCF limitations (single risk-adjusted discount rate) by separating risky revenue from relatively stable costs.

Value: Reveals option value in volatile resource projects and improves investment decisions.

Energy, Climate & Carbon Advisory

  • Carbon content calculation (project and portfolio footprints).
  • Carbon pricing & offsets — market evaluation and integration into project economics.
  • Levelised Cost of Energy (LCOE) calculation to compare renewable and conventional generation.
  • Abatement cost curves & scenarios to identify least-cost emission reductions.
  • Energy policy and transition economics, including fiscal and market impacts.

Value: Cost-effective pathways to manage carbon risk and attract sustainable finance.

Market & Strategic Advisory

  • Supply–demand outlooks for minerals and energy.
  • Competition analysis (HHI) and strategy for positioning in PNG and regional markets.
  • Portfolio strategy across commodities, including rare earths and transition minerals.

Value: Supports long-term strategy and competitive positioning.

Corporate Finance & Growth Advisory

  • Debt and equity structuring for resource projects (including free-carried interests).
  • M&A due diligence, valuation, and deal support.
  • Capital raising scenarios and shareholder value analysis.

Value: Helps secure financing and deliver sustainable growth.

Governance & Boardroom Advisory

  • Governance protocols and board decision support.
  • Balancing industry, community, and government interests in resource development.
  • Change management, negotiation, and communication for complex results.

Value: Strengthens executive credibility and stakeholder alignment.
