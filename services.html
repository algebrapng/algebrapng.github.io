Detailed Services

Economic Modelling & Forecasting

  • Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) and Input–Output (I–O) modelling to evaluate government policy, taxation, subsidies, and investment impacts.
  • Econometrics & forecasting for macroeconomic indicators, sector output, and commodity prices.
  • Mineral market analysis & forecasting, including Herfindahl–Hirschman Index (HHI) for competition assessment.

Value: Evidence-based insights into how policies and projects shape PNG’s economy.

Project Appraisal & Investment Analysis

  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) and Net Present Value (NPV) evaluation of mining, energy, and infrastructure projects.
  • Cost of capital & discount rate analysis (WACC, sovereign and financing risk).
  • Decision trees to compare risk-neutral and risk-averse investment pathways.
  • LCOE-linked appraisals where energy cost drives project viability.

Value: Transparent, robust investment appraisals aligned to real-world risk.

Modern Asset Pricing & Real Options Valuation

  • Modern Asset Pricing (MAP) to capture commodity volatility and separate risky revenues from more stable costs.
  • Real Options Valuation (ROV) to recognise the value of flexibility (expand, delay, suspend, abandon).
  • Use of stochastic models, binomial lattices, and replicating portfolio methods for realistic valuation.
  • Address DCF limitations by adding option value to traditional project evaluations.

Value: Reveals hidden option value in volatile projects, improving investment decisions.

Energy, Climate & Carbon Advisory

  • Carbon content calculation for projects and portfolios.
  • Carbon pricing & offsets — evaluate offset markets and integrate pricing into project economics.
  • Levelised Cost of Energy (LCOE) calculation to benchmark renewable and conventional energy projects.
  • Abatement cost curves & scenarios to identify least-cost emission reduction options.
  • Energy policy & transition economics, including fiscal and market impacts.

Value: Cost-effective pathways to manage carbon risk and align with sustainable finance requirements.

Market & Strategic Advisory

  • Supply–demand outlooks for minerals and energy, including transition minerals.
  • Competition analysis (HHI) and industry strategy.
  • Portfolio strategy across commodities to support resilience and long-term growth.

Value: Supports long-term strategy and competitive positioning.

Corporate Finance & Growth

  • Independent analysis to support financing decisions in resource and energy projects.
  • Apply option pricing and real options modelling to inform mergers, acquisitions, and partnerships.
  • Assist in assessing capital raising options through evidence-based scenarios (debt, equity, or hybrids).

Value: Provides transparent, model-driven insights for financing strategies and growth opportunities.

Policy & Decision-Support Advisory

  • Translate complex models into clear, actionable insights for ministers, boards, and communities.
  • Distributional impact analysis across households, industries, and regions.
  • Balance economic, social, and environmental objectives in policy design and evaluation.

Value: Builds trust and transparency in decision-making processes.
