Our Services

We keep the detail straightforward here. Each service can be tailored to your needs, with technical methods and models provided in proposals when helpful.

Economic Modelling & Forecasting

Clear, evidence-based analysis of PNG’s economy, markets, and competition.

  • Macroeconomic and sector forecasts; scenario analysis and stress-testing.
  • Economy-wide impact asessment.
  • Econometrics for policy evaluation and market/price dynamics.
  • Competition and market structure assessments.

Value: Turns data and models into actionable insights for planning and policy.

Project Appraisal & Investment Analysis

Transparent evaluations of project viability and risk for public and private decisions.

  • DCF/NPV and cost-of-capital assessments aligned to PNG risk context.
  • Decision trees and scenario frameworks for choices under uncertainty.
  • Integration with energy cost drivers (e.g., LCOE) when relevant.

Value: Supports sound investment choices, grounded in real-world risk.

Energy, Climate & Carbon Advisory

Practical guidance that balances growth, sustainability, and finance requirements.

  • Carbon accounting and pricing strategies, including offsets.
  • Levelised Cost of Energy (LCOE) benchmarks for renewable and conventional options.
  • Abatement cost curves and transition scenarios for least-cost pathways.
  • Energy policy and market analysis, including fiscal impacts.

Value: Cost-effective pathways to manage carbon and meet sustainability goals.

Strategy, Governance & Policy

Independent advice to strengthen institutions, transparency, and long-term strategy.

  • Long-term outlooks and portfolio strategy across sectors.
  • Financing strategies and capital-raising options analysis.
  • Policy design, evaluation, and distributional impact analysis.
  • Decision-support for ministers, boards, and development partners.

Value: Builds trust in decision-making and supports resilient, inclusive growth.
