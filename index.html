Our Key Services

Economic Modelling & Forecasting

CGE and I–O modelling, econometrics, mineral market analysis and forecasting, and competition assessment using the HHI Index.

Project Appraisal & Investment Analysis

DCF and NPV evaluation, cost of capital and discount rate analysis, and decision trees to assess risk under uncertainty.

Modern Asset Pricing & Real Options

Application of MAP and ROV to capture volatility and flexibility in mining and energy projects for more accurate valuations.

Energy, Climate & Carbon Advisory

Carbon content calculation, carbon pricing and offsets, Levelised Cost of Energy (LCOE) calculation, and abatement cost analysis.

Market & Strategic Advisory

Supply–demand outlooks, competition intensity analysis, and portfolio strategies across minerals and energy sectors.

Corporate Finance & Growth

Independent analysis to support financing decisions in resource and energy projects. Application of option pricing and real options modelling to inform mergers, acquisitions, and capital raising strategies.

Policy & Decision-Support Advisory

Independent, transparent advice to simplify complex modelling for decision-makers. Support for government, industry, and development partners to balance economic, social, and environmental outcomes.
